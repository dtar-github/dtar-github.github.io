About Me

Hello! You might know me as DevDoors or dtar. I like to code, make redstone projects, make music, and do math. I make games and simulations on Scratch, design intricate redstone contraptions in Minecraft, and create math art and visualizations on Desmos.

On my website, you'll find redstone resources, Desmos graphs, and more! You can also play and download some of my games and simulations. Don't miss out on exploring my Minecraft server, DevCraft, where everything runs on command blocks and redstone.
