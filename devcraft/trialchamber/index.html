Trial Chambers: Expansion

Trial Chambers: Expansion is a dungeon crawler built as an expansion to the Trial Chambers. They are a system of challenging dungeons designed to test player skill and strategy. Each level is structured to push players through increasingly difficult encounters, encouraging thoughtful combat, and risk-versus-reward decision-making.

As players progress through the chambers, they face a wide variety of custom enemies, each with unique behaviors, strengths, and mechanics that require adaptation rather than brute force. Encounters are designed to scale in difficulty, ensuring that runs demand stronger gear, positioning, and strategy.

Progression in Trial Chambers: Expansion is heavily tied to dungeon completion. Successful runs reward players with powerful gear, upgrades, and rare items that enhance future attempts and open new playstyles.

The Trial Chambers serve as both a test and a progression path, rewarding players who are willing to push further, take calculated risks, and master the mechanics of the game. Whether entering for steady progression or attempting high-level challenges, players are constantly encouraged to improve, adapt, and refine their approach as they descend deeper into the chambers.

Trial Chambers Expansion Guide: Bosses

The Marionettist

The Marionettist is the final boss of the 3rd level.

Stages

Stage 1:

Three example minions.

Stage 2:

Three Skirmishers and a Brute.

Stage 3:

Five Forgotten Vanguards.

Stage 4:

The Mage Youngling and its summons.

Stage 5:

Four Forgotten Vanguards, four Skirmishers, two Gloomstep Assassins, and one Brute.

Health: 775hp
Waves: 5

The Geomancer

The Geomancer is the final boss of level 4 and the final form of the Mage Youngling.

Attacks

Fangs

Summons an array of fangs on nearby players.

Floating Blocks

Raises floating terrain that hurts nearby players.

Health: 100hp
Fang Attack Strength: 6 Attack Damage
Floating Block Attack Strength: 9 Attack Damage

Trial Chambers Expansion Guide: Custom Mobs

Bogged Lord

The Bogged Lord is a keyholder in the chambers. It is a very tanky mob that shoots strong poison arrows. It also has a chance to drop some of its Mud Armor when killed.

Starlight Skeleton Master

The Starlight Skeleton Master is a keyholder in the chambers. It shoots strong arrows that deal a lot of knockback.

Starlight Skeleton

The Starlight Skeleton is a mob within the chambers. It is a hard hitting mob that is typically found defending its master. It also has a 1.5% chance to drop a Starlight Sword when killed.

Spider King

The Spider King is a keyholder in the chambers. It is a tanky mob that is typically found among other spiders.

Wither Miner

The Wither Miner is a mob within the chambers and is typically found in large groups.

Illusioner

The illusioner is a keyholder within the chambers. This is an unreleased mob that is within vanilla Minecraft but cannot be found in survival.

Forgotten Vanguard

The Forgotten Vanguard is a mob within the chambers that is one of The Marionettist's puppets. It also has a 2.5% chance to drop a Vanguard's Shroud.

Skirmisher

The Skirmisher is a mob within the chambers that is one of The Marionettist's puppets.

Mage Youngling

The Mage Youngling is an apprentice of The Marionettist and later evolves into the Geomancer.

Gloomstep Assassin

The Gloomstep Assassin is a mob within the chambers that is one of The Marionettist's puppets.

Brute

The Brute is a mob within the chambers. It is a modified ravager with special abilities and is one of The Marionettist's puppets.
