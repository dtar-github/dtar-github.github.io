Grapple Cat

To grapple:

1. Move your mouse to where you want to go

2. Press down until grapple attached

3. Let go of the trackpad/mouse


To swing:

1. Point the grapple at a black dot

2. Press down until grapple attached and keep holding

3. Hold the up arrow until your cat is high enough

4. Let go of the up arrow

5. Let go of the trackpad/mouse
