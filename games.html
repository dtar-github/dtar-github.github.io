Grapple Cat ⬇  — View Code

To grapple:

1. Move your mouse to where you want to go

2. Press down until grapple attached

3. Let go of the trackpad/mouse


To swing:

1. Point the grapple at a black dot

2. Press down until grapple attached and keep holding

3. Hold the up arrow until your cat is high enough

4. Let go of the up arrow

5. Let go of the trackpad/mouse

If you get stuck or it glitches out, press the green flag again (it will save your current level)

Some momentum techniques:

• Shoot a portal on the wall and on the ground and jump through twice

• Shoot a portal on the ceiling and on the ground for an infinite loop

• Shoot two portals on the ground (one elevated) and jump from the higher one to the lower one
